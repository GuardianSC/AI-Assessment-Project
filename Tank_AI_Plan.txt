Cannon States:

	STATIONARY-Cannon doesn't move
	
	LOOK-Cannon rotates in a circle looking for a target
	
	AIM-When cannon detects a target, cannon tracks it
	
	FIRE-After a few seconds of tracking the target, cannon fires. Try to get it to predict where target will move to lead the shot for better chance of a successful shot

	PANICFIRE-Cannon randomly fires a few shots

	
Tank States:
	MOVEFORWARD-Tank moves forward
	
	MOVEBACKWARD-Tank moves backward
	
	TURNLEFT-Tank turns left
	
	TURNRIGHT-Tank turns right
	
	STOP-Tank doesn't move
		
	INVESTIGATE-When an enemy is out of sight amd fires, detect the 			    direction and move towards it
